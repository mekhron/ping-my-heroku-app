This app is for education purposes, so it has some restrictions:
  • Your app has to be UP and running, before you add it to pinger
  • It will keep pinging your app only for 3 days. If you need extend it, you have to come back and extend it manually;
  • It is only for heroku apps, links to other apps will be ignored;
  • You can add only 3 apps from your IP.
